Gamification

One of the newer strategies in the consumer behavior industry is Gamification, which is the practice of applying behavioral change concepts from games to improve products and services. Many products and services have utilized this to improve user experience, increase user retention, and change consumer behavior showing how gamification is the future of customer engagement.

Consumer Insights

Based on trend analysis and human behavior analysis, insights into consumer behavior can give your company the edge it may need to grow as a business. Some of the largest consumer-driven companies are in Cincinnati and they collect data on consumers across the nation. If you are looking for insights into consumer behavior then Cincinnati is where you want to be.
